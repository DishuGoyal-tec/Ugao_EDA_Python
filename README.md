# 🌿 Ugaoo E-Commerce Sales Analysis

This project presents an **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** of Ugaoo’s e-commerce sales data to uncover key insights related to customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and profitability drivers.

The analysis focuses on identifying patterns in sales transactions and understanding how factors such as **product categories, regional demand, payment methods, and discount strategies** impact overall business performance.

---

## 📊 Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this analysis are:

- Identify **top-performing states and cities** contributing to revenue and profit
- Understand **product category performance**
- Analyze **customer payment preferences**
- Examine the **impact of discounts on profitability**
- Detect potential **operational risks such as high-return transactions**

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **e-commerce transaction data from Ugaoo**, a gardening and plant-based online retail brand.

Key features included in the dataset:

- Order ID
- Product Category
- City and State
- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Order Value
- Discount
- Final Price
- Profit
- Payment Mode
- Delivery Type

Each row represents a **single transaction**.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

Some of the major insights identified from the analysis include:

- Profit contribution is **highly concentrated in a few states**, with Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh contributing a significant share.
- **Flowering Plants and Outdoor Plants** emerge as the most profitable product categories.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD)** accounts for the largest share of orders but is associated with relatively higher return tendencies.
- Increasing discount levels show a **negative impact on average profit margins**.
- High-value transactions combined with large discounts indicate **potential operational risk and revenue leakage**.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies can improve business performance:

- Expand successful sales strategies from **high-performing regions** to other markets.
- Prioritize **high-margin product categories** in marketing and inventory planning.
- Encourage **prepaid payment methods** to reduce return-related operational costs.
- Implement **controlled discount strategies** to protect profit margins.
- Monitor **high-value discounted orders** to reduce potential return risks.
